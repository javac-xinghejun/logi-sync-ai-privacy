Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

Introduction

Logi Sync AI ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

Logi Sync AI stores all data locally on your device using your browser's IndexedDB:

  • Saved AI Responses - The content of AI responses you choose to save
  • Conversation Metadata - Platform (ChatGPT/Claude), message timestamp, and conversation title
  • Tags and Projects - Labels and organizational categories you create
  • Settings - Your preferences and configuration

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ No personal identification information (name, email, IP address)
  • ❌ No usage analytics or tracking
  • ❌ No data sent to external servers
  • ❌ No third-party cookies or trackers

Data Storage and Retention

  • Location: All data is stored locally on your device in your browser's IndexedDB
  • Duration: Data remains until you delete it or uninstall the extension
  • Backup: You can export your saved data as Markdown files at any time
  • Sync: No cloud sync or backup functionality (all data stays on your device)

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties. Your saved AI responses are never transmitted to any server.

Data Access and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View: Access all saved responses through the extension's management page
  • Edit: Modify tags, projects, and content
  • Delete: Remove individual saved responses or delete all data
  • Export: Download your saved responses as Markdown files
  • Clear Data: Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data

Third-Party Services

Logi Sync AI does not use any third-party services that collect or process your data.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored locally using IndexedDB with browser-level security
  • No network requests are made with your saved content
  • The extension operates only within approved domains (ChatGPT, Claude)

Children's Privacy

Logi Sync AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object
